Our Model

Professional Freedom & Shared Excellence

We understand that running a private practice can feel isolating. Our clinic-within-a-clinic, rent-based model gives you everything you need to focus on what you do best — while remaining completely your own boss.

🗝️
Autonomy

Maintain full control over your schedule, client load, and clinical approach. Your practice, your way — always.

🛋️
Professional Space

Beautifully designed, fully furnished therapy rooms — thoughtfully appointed for individuals, couples, or families.

🏷️
Your Identity

Benefit from the credibility of an established clinic brand while fully maintaining your own independent business identity.

🤝
Referral Network

Join a community of specialized associates, creating opportunities for internal referrals and collegial consultation.

What We Look For

Practitioners who share
our values

We welcome clinicians who bring their own strengths and style, while sharing our warm, client-centred approach and a genuine commitment to growth — personally and professionally.

  • Registration with the CRPO or equivalent body, in good standing
  • A Master's degree in Counselling, Social Work, Psychology, or equivalent
  • Experience working with a range of clients and presenting concerns
  • Commitment to evidence-informed, ethical, and compassionate practice
  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills
  • Professional liability insurance through a recognized organization
  • A collaborative spirit and openness to peer consultation

What's Included

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Our rent-based model removes the administrative burden of finding and maintaining professional space — so you can show up fully for your clients every single session.

You rent only the days and times you need, with 60 days notice by either party should circumstances change.

📅
Flexible Rental
Choose the specific days and times that work for your practice. Scale up or down as your caseload evolves — with 60 days notice by either party.
🌐
Digital Presence
A dedicated profile on our Associates page, with direct links to your own booking platform so clients can reach you directly.
🏢
Fully Furnished Offices
Thoughtfully designed, professionally furnished therapy rooms suitable for individual, parent, or family sessions — ready from day one.
📶
Full Amenities
High-speed internet, secure building access, and a calm private waiting area to welcome your clients comfortably.
💬
Collegial Community
Access to a peer network of like-minded clinicians for informal consultation, referrals, and the kind of professional connection that sustains great practice.
How to Apply

Take the
next step

If you're ready to grow your practice within a collaborative, empowering environment — we would genuinely love to hear from you. The process is straightforward and conversational.

1
Reach out to Sylvia
Send an email introducing yourself — who you are, your training background, and what draws you to Success in Steps.
2
Share your CV or résumé
Include a brief note outlining your areas of focus, your therapeutic approach, and your availability.
3
Come for a conversation & tour
If there's a potential fit, Sylvia will invite you to visit the clinic, see the space in person, and have an open conversation about what this arrangement could look like for you.
4
Welcome to the team
Once details are confirmed, you'll be set up with your profile, office access, and everything you need to begin seeing clients.
