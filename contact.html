Common Questions

Before you reach out

A few answers to questions we hear often.

Yes — we offer a complimentary 15-minute phone consultation for all new clients. This is a no-pressure conversation to help us understand what you're looking for and make sure we're the right fit before you commit to a session.

Absolutely. We offer secure video sessions to clients anywhere in Ontario. Many clients appreciate the flexibility and privacy of virtual therapy — it's just as effective as in-person for most concerns.

Many extended health benefit plans cover psychotherapy with a Registered Psychotherapist (RP) or Registered Social Worker (RSW). We recommend checking with your provider before your first session. We provide official receipts for all sessions for submission to your insurer.

Standard sessions are 50 minutes. Longer sessions of 80 minutes can be arranged for family therapy or when a client prefers extended time. We'll discuss what works best for you during your consultation.

That's exactly what our free consultation is for. You don't need to have it all figured out — just reach out and share what's on your mind. We'll listen, ask a few questions, and help guide you toward the right fit. You can also explore our services page for more detail on each offering.

Yes. Confidentiality is a cornerstone of our practice. All clinicians at Success in Steps are bound by their professional regulatory bodies' codes of ethics. Information is only shared in limited legally required circumstances (such as risk of serious harm), which your therapist will discuss with you in your first session.
