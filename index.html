Meet Your Therapist

Sylvia Corzato,
RP, MACP

With over 25 years of combined experience as a psychotherapist, parent coach, and behaviour therapist, Sylvia holds a Master's in Counselling Psychology with distinction from Yorkville University — and brings both professional depth and lived parenting experience to every client relationship.

Founder of Success in Steps since 2016, Sylvia is a recognized voice on parenting and mental health in Ottawa. She is a regular contributor to CTV Morning Live Ottawa, and has been featured in the Globe and Mail, CBC, Today's Parent, and Parenting Times.

Our Approach

How we
work with you

Our practice is built on four commitments that shape every session — regardless of which clinician you work with.

Tailored

No two clients are alike. Every plan of care is built around your specific goals, strengths, and life circumstances — never from a template.

Evidence-Informed

We draw on established therapeutic approaches — CBT, ACT, EFFT, and more — selecting what the research shows works best for your situation.

Compassionate

Healing happens in safe, non-judgmental relationship. Whatever you bring into the room will be met with warmth and complete acceptance.

Collaborative

You are the expert on your own life. Our role is to walk beside you — to offer tools, perspective, and presence — not to prescribe a path.

The Experience

What it's like to
work with us

Reaching out takes courage. From your very first session, our goal is to make the process feel approachable, meaningful, and genuinely yours.

  • A pace that feels comfortable
    We follow your lead — there is no rush, no pressure, and no timeline you must meet.
  • Practical tools from day one
    Sessions are grounded in real strategies you can bring into your everyday life immediately.
  • A fully non-judgmental space
    Whatever you bring through the door is met with warmth, curiosity, and complete acceptance.
  • A true collaboration
    Your therapist is an active partner in your growth — working with you, not just observing from a distance.
